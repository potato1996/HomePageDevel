About Me

Hi, this is Potato, a technology lover, a geek and nerd in computer scicence.
Well my life of programming started when I was 12. Just like many other teenager boys, I loved play computer games = =. I didn't have money to buy fancy 3A games at that time, instead all I had was only flash games or web MMORPGS - which were easy to write hack tools and/or robots, and that was what I did. Then I started with Visual Basic and merely some imcomplete knowledge of memory and network stuffs that I learnt from hacker forums.
In senior high I got my first own PC. Then I got to be a fan in hardware. I've tried lots of cheap and geeky stuffs (like E3-1200 series:P), but finally end up with the pressure of GaoKao...
Anyway that's why I choose CS as my major during the college where I got a deep dive in to this field, and why I got in loved with low level stuffs. Although CS was not completly the same thing that I imagine - obviously there was no courses told me how to become a hacker - I found the things that interesed and amazed enough for me - the beauty of complexity and delicateness in OS, the force of computation in HPC, and power of spell in DL.
The story ends here and the rest of stuffs are on my CV :P
